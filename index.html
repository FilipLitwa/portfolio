About Me

I’m a UX designer dedicated to creating intuitive, engaging digital experiences. I hold a degree in Social Informatics from AGH University of Science and Technology, where I explored the intersection of technology, design, and human behavior. My experience includes working on real-world projects, from research and prototyping to usability testing and interface design in Figma. I strive to design digital products that not only function seamlessly but also enhance the way people connect and interact.

BoardGameArena – Case Study

Redesigned a popular board-gaming platform by modernizing its outdated interface, enhancing visual hierarchy, and significantly improving overall readability. The project included a detailed UX redesign process involving user interviews, journey mapping, and iterative prototyping to identify and resolve key usability pain points, resulting in clearer navigation, more intuitive user flows, and improved player engagement and retention.

Plantopia – Plant Care App

An intelligent plant care application featuring an interactive plant map that visually connects species with similar needs, such as light level, water requirements, and soil type. The app supports plant growth by offering personalized care tips and allows users to create a virtual garden to better understand and manage their plants.

Hearthbeat – Event Design & Interaction App

Design of a social event planning app that combines scheduling with social interaction. The prototype features a drag-and-drop interaction map for structuring events, assigning roles, and sharing tasks. Designed through design thinking, with personas, user scenarios, and usability testing, the app balances planning tools with collaborative and social features, making it adaptable to both casual meetups and large-scale events.
